# GastroTwin: The AI-Powered Financial Digital Twin for Restaurants

GastroTwin is an infrastructure intelligence platform that eliminates intra-month financial blindness in the food and hospitality industry. Instead of managing restaurants with a "rearview mirror" (checking traditional accounting 15 days after the month ends), GastroTwin acts as a real-time operational engine that calculates daily profitability, dynamic break-even points, and uncovers recoverable leakage before the day finishes.

## ⚡ The Problem
The restaurant industry operates on razor-thin net margins. Operational managers lose millions due to manual invoice tracking, unexpected staffing costs, and price variance from suppliers. Because consolidating POS data, labor clocks, and physical invoices takes hours of manual spreadsheets, managers only see their true margin when it's too late to correct the course.

## 🎯 The Solution: 5-Layer Management Infrastructure
1. **Daily P&G (Core):** Real-time tracking of daily revenue against dynamic Prime Cost (Food, Beverage + Labor) and prorated fixed expenses.
2. **Dynamic Break-Even Point:** A daily shifting threshold based on historical sales curves and automated staffing costs.
3. **Actionable Alerts:** Gemini-powered proactive insights instead of passive charts.
4. **Operational Simulator (Digital Twin):** A low-friction sandbox where owners can live-test adjustments.
5. **Leakage Recovery Dashboard:** A direct monetization sheet mapping actionable operational changes to immediate cash return.

## 🤖 AI Agent Architecture (Build with Gemini)
GastroTwin leverages Google Cloud and Gemini Models to automate the hardest piece of the daily P&G puzzle: **Cost Ingestion**.
* **Cost Parser Agent (Gemini + Document AI):** Allows restaurant managers to simply snap a photo of physical supplier invoices. The agent instantly extracts items, units, total values, and categories.
* **Predictive Simulation Agent:** Models financial outcomes and probability vectors for the operational simulator layer.
